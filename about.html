About COER University

COER University is a private state university located in Roorkee, Uttarakhand, India, founded in 1998 as the College of Engineering Roorkee (COER) by the Seth Roshan Lal Jain Trust. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across fields like engineering, health sciences, management, and agriculture within its 75-acre campus. The university emphasizes research, innovation, and all-around student development through a strong focus on modern facilities, international collaborations, and a curriculum aligned with the National Education Policy 2020.
